Michael Franti is an American musician who has made a name for himself as a singer, songwriter, rapper, poet, and social activist. Franti is known for his diverse musical style, blending elements of hip-hop, reggae, rock, folk, and pop.

Franti initially gained recognition as a member of the band The Beatnigs and later formed the group Spearhead in 1994. With Spearhead, he has released numerous albums that often feature socially conscious and politically charged lyrics, addressing topics such as war, injustice, love, and resilience.

Throughout his career, Franti has been an outspoken advocate for peace, social justice, and environmental sustainability. His music often reflects his activism and promotes messages of love, unity, and positive change.

The song ***I Know I'm Not Alone*** can be interpreted as a reflection on the changes and challenges that have occurred over time, evoking nostalgia for a time when things were simpler and more joyful. The sun can be seen as a metaphor for happiness or optimism that seemed more constant in the past.

The lyrics express a sense of disillusionment and the perception that things have changed for the worse. His reference to seasons not coming on time suggests a lack of predictability or balance in the world, while the mention of war and anger highlights the negative impact of conflict and negative emotions on society.

Confusion and disillusionment arises from the constant influx of news and information, while the reference to being told to choose among lies implies a lack of trust in the narratives presented. The mention of loss, violence, and the impact on families conveys the gravity of the situation and the questioning of what has happened to our society.

*But I know, I know, I know I'm not alone* - these repeated lines convey a sense of hope and unity amidst the challenges. It suggests that even though there are difficulties, there are others who share similar experiences and emotions, providing a sense of support and solidarity.

Overall, the song seems to convey a mix of longing for a simpler, more joyful past, alongside a recognition of the challenges and disillusionment of the present. It reflects on the impact of conflict, misinformation, and loss in society while also emphasizing the importance of finding support and unity in difficult times.
