   - Comparison of the effort put into investigating Trump versus addressing local crime.

**III. Crime in New York and Public Response**

1. **Rampant Crime**
   - Issues faced by bodega shopkeepers and ordinary citizens.

   - Explanation of the perception that criminals are not adequately punished.
3. **Comparison to Political Cases**
   - Contrasting the legal pursuit of Trump with the lack of attention to everyday crime.

**IV. Strategic Importance of Trial Venue**

1. **Selection of Favorable Venues**
   - How venues are chosen for strategic advantage in legal cases.
2. **High-Profile Examples**

3. **Implications for Justice**
   - How venue selection can impact the outcomes of trials.

**V. Plausible Points of Departure**

1. **Ethics of Using Legal Means for Political Ends**
   - Whether it is justified to use legal cases to influence elections.

2. **Impact on Democracy**
   - Potential consequences of undermining legal principles to "save" democracy.

3. **Balancing Political and Social Justice**
   - How to address both high-profile political cases and everyday crime.

4. **Public Trust in Legal Systems**
   - How the focus on political cases affects public confidence in the justice system.

5. **Economic Impact on Local Businesses**
   - The effect of crime on small businesses in areas like New York.

6. **Judicial Bias and Fairness**
   - Concerns about impartiality in the judicial process due to venue selection.

7. **Role of Media in Shaping Public Opinion**
   - How media coverage influences perceptions of justice and political cases.

8. **Long-Term Consequences for Political Figures**

9. **Legal Precedents and Future Cases**
   - How current cases set precedents for future legal actions.

10. **Policy Changes to Address Crime and Justice**
   - Possible reforms to address both crime and perceived injustices in the legal system.
