This is addressed to every person who holds public trust and has watched the killing repeat.

You have two mechanisms. They are specific, implementable, and politically viable across the partisan divide because neither touches the hardware debate. They operate upstream of the instrument at the informational and architectural level.

The first is the **Anchor-Dense Zone** pilot program. Designate three municipalities for a two-year ADZ cohort. Provide municipal infrastructure grants tied to measurable Presence Ratio improvement: co-location hours per resident per week against a documented baseline. Measure the P-S-F Matrix indicators before and after. The pilot produces a dataset. The dataset produces a replication model. The replication model scales. The legislative cost is a grant program. The return is a measurable reduction in isolation-driven vulnerability at the population level.

The second is the **Voluntary Transparency Standard** legislative endorsement. You do not write the VTS into law. You co-sign the market mechanism that makes it financially rational for platforms to adopt it voluntarily. A joint statement by a bipartisan coalition of legislators endorsing VTS adoption moves the ESG calculation immediately. Institutional investors read legislative signals. The statement costs nothing to produce and shifts the financial incentive structure the moment it publishes.

The digital archive does not expire. The same script that generated the first post-1999 event is searchable today. The next person in isolation will find it in under a second. The cost of continued inaction is measured in school hallways, transit platforms, and community centers across every district you represent.

The ADZ pilot and the VTS endorsement are two actionable decisions available this legislative session. The Notoriety Arbitrage framework is the evidentiary foundation for both. The mechanism is named. The intervention points are mapped. The dataset supports the investment.

Contact your legislative office today and ask what ADZ or VTS proposals are currently on the docket.

Read the full economic framework: [Notoriety Arbitrage (DiBella, 2026)](https://bikepaths.org/topics/white_papers/notoriety_arbitrage_informational_violence.pdf).

**Glossary**
**Anchor-Dense Zone (ADZ)**: A municipally designated geographic area engineered for high co-presence density through infrastructure investment tied to Presence Ratio measurement.
**Voluntary Transparency Standard (VTS)**: The market-based platform protocol that collapses Notoriety Yield through ESG-routed financial incentives without requiring legislation.
